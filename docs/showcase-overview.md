# NGDXH Showcase Overview

## What NGDXH Is

NGDXH, the National Government Data Exchange Hub, is a working prototype of a government interoperability layer for Uganda. It connects existing ministry and agency systems rather than replacing them.

The prototype demonstrates how a citizen or organization can be profiled across multiple government domains through controlled exchange, policy-based access, and audit logging.

## What It Demonstrates

- Public citizen-facing services
- Secure officer sign-in and ministry dashboards
- Role-based access control for internal users
- Company verification workflows for employers and institutions
- NIRA-led biometric verification workflows
- Audit receipts and simulated SSO session handling

## Public Versus Internal

The public side is intentionally limited to guidance, service status, citizen tools, and company entry points.

Internal operational tools are kept behind officer sign-in and are role-scoped by ministry.

## Core Government Value

NGDXH supports a national exchange model where authorized systems can:

- verify identity through NIRA
- check tax compliance through URA
- check immigration and visa status through DCIC
- check land, health, transport, education, police, and telecom-related records through their respective domains
- produce a single controlled view for authorized workflows

## Current Prototype Status

This repository is a working prototype and showcase build. It is suitable for demonstrating the workflow, security model, and system concept.

It is not a replacement for ministry systems and does not claim live production integration.
